Info:

This patch is meant to be used alongside Craftable Horse Barding.

The "open inventory" option from Immersive Horses acts just like the "search" option from Requiem (when used on a horse). Use whatever method you prefer.



Load Order:

Must be loaded after "Requiem - Minor Arcana - Roleplaying.esp".

Should be loaded after "Requiem - SkyTEST.esp". Manual conflict resolution is advised for advanced users.

Should be loaded after "Requiem - Wild World.esp". Manual conflict resolution is advised for advanced users.
